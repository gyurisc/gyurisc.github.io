It occurred to me this morning, how I am not keeping any coins in my pocket anymore. Not wearing watches. No cameras with me, not using alarm clocks. 
There are no coins in my pocket, because it would scratch the gorilla glass on my smartphone. Watch, camera and alarm clocks are obsoleted by the phone! 
No shreds of paper and pen with me to write down things.


The smartphone took over and I am getting old and nostalgic now!
