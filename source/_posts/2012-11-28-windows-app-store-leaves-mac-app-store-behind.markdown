{% blockquote Distimo http://www.distimo.com/blog/2012_11_publication-discover-the-windows-store/ Discover The Windows Store%}
The daily download volume among the top 300 most popular applications in the Windows Store is already three times higher than the volume among the top 300 most popular applications in the Apple Mac App Store. However, in terms of paid volume only, the Apple Mac App Store is still doing much (5 times) better.
{% endblockquote %}

After one month, Windows Store has 3 times more downloads in the top 300 apps than the app store shipping with the [world's most advanced desktop operating system](http://www.apple.com/osx/). Not too bad, although the Mac App Store still generates 5 times more paid volume than Windows Store. 

It's a start!
